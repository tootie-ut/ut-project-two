 ## Ingredients
 
 
 1. 2 tbsp tamarind
 2. 125ml hot water
 
 3. 2 tsp chilli powder
 4. 1 kg chuck steak
 
 5. 10-15 curry leaves
 6. 2 onions
 7. 100g ghee
 8. 6 green chillis
 9. 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
 10. 2 tsp tumeric
 11. 1 tsp cumin
 12. 1 tsp  ground black pepper
 13. 2 cloves garlic
 14. 1 bunch coriander stalk
 15. 2 tbsp tomato paste
 
 16. 250ml beef stock
